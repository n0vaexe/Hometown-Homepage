Visit Payson

Enjoy hiking and local produce in this quaint, small town in southern Utah County

Top three activities to do in Payson

Hike up Payson Canyon

The Grotto Trail is an easy path with beautiful views and a waterfall at the end.

Swim at the city pool

Payson Community Pool is a fun way to spend your afternoon with the whole family.

Dine at Tadka

Tadka Cuisine of India offers a wide variety of delicious entrees.
